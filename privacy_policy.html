If you reside outside India, your information will be handled, processed, stored, used, stored and shared as per applicable Indian privacy laws and standards. For the purpose of this Privacy Policy, wherever the context so requires “you” or “your” shall mean User and the term “we”, “us”, “our” shall mean Circled. This Privacy Policy should be read along with the Terms & Conditions of the Website (available at: www.circled.co.in). Your information is collected by us at the time of your access or browsing of the Website, registration on the Website, or any other action taken by you on the Website. It is important that you read this Privacy Policy together with any other notice we may provide on specific occasions when we are collecting or processing personal information about you so that you are fully aware of how and why we are using your personal information. This Privacy Policy supplements any and all other such notices and is not intended to override them. All words and expressions used and not defined in this Privacy Policy shall have meanings assigned to them in the Information Technology Act, 2000, the Information Technology (Reasonable security practices and procedures and sensitive personal data or information) Rules, 2011 and the Terms and Conditions.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT ABOUT YOU AND MANNER OF COLLECTION

Personal Data means any information that relates to a natural person, which, either directly or indirectly, in combination with other information available or likely to be available, is capable of identifying such natural person. Personal data does not include anonymized or aggregated data which cannot identify an individual. Personal data consists of ‘sensitive personal data or information’ which consists of financial data and information relating to a natural person. We may collect, use, store and transfer the following types of Personal Data which is voluntarily provided by you:

  • Personal and business contact information including name, postal and email address, Aadhaar number and telephone/mobile number.
  • Tax status (pan card, tax ID, tax residence).
  • Financial data (assets, investor profile, credit history, bank account details, demat account details, debts and expenses).
  • Other background data(information on unlawful conduct/criminal history).
  • Technical data including internet protocol (IP) address, your login data, browser type and version, time zone setting and location, browser plug-in types and versions, operating system and platform and other technology on the devices you use to access this Website.

By providing your Personal Data, you consent to our use of your Personal Data in accordance with this Privacy Policy. You can withdraw your consent to our use of your Personal Data, at any time, by writing to Circled’s Privacy Officer (as per details provided below). If you withdraw your consent to our use of your Personal Data, Circled shall not be obligated to provide its services to you or permit you to access our Website.

We use different methods to collect data from and about you including through:

  • Website use and registration: Personal Data may be provided by you, as appropriate, when contacting Circled through the Website.
  • Automated technologies or interactions.

Information automatically collected: As you interact with our Website, we may automatically collect certain information of technical nature including, without limitation, your internet protocol (“IP”) address, information about the equipment on which you access the Website, information about the browser, operating system, and your browsing actions and patterns, to improve the user experience on the Website. Automatic collection of such information is facilitated with the use of “cookies”, server logs and/ or other similar digital tools for collection of such information. We may also receive such information about you if you visit other websites employing our cookies. Please note that use of cookies or similar digital tools for collection of such information by us does not result in gaining access to information that is capable of personally identifying you, i.e. Personal Information/ Sensitive Personal Data. You can opt to refuse all or some browser cookies when prompted on our Website about our use of cookies. If you disable or refuse our use of cookies, please note that all or some parts of this Website may become inaccessible or not function properly. Information collected from other sources: Other than the information you grant us directly, we may also gain access to your information from third-parties you have provided your information to and who we have partnered with for the provision of services to you on the Website. We may also gain access to your information from publicly available sources.
